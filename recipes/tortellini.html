Tortellini

Description

A recipe for creamy, delicious tortellini. A real crowd pleaser you can eat as is or add protein.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 4 ounces softened cream cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup fresh parmesan, grated
  • 1 cup fresh asiago, grated
  • 1 package Buitoni Mixed Cheese Tortellini

Instructions

  1. In large pot bring 3 quarts of water to boil. Add package of tortellini. Reduce heat and boil gently.
  2. Add butter, heavy whipping cream and cream cheese to medium saucepan. Whisk over medium heat until well combined.
  3. Add garlic, oregano, basil and black pepper. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Add asiago and parmesan. Whisk continually until well combined. Bring to a simmer and whisk until desired thickness.
  5. Check tortellini for softness. Drain into colander then toss with alfredo sauce.
  6. Enjoy!
