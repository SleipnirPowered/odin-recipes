Beef Stew

Description

My favorite beef stew recipe. Perfect for a rainy day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp of bacon fat (or butter)
  • 2 lb beef chuck, cubed
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb baby or red potatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup of pearled barley
  • 4 tsp of Better Than Bouillon
  • 6 cups water
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 6oz can tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp bacon fat (or butter) in large dutch oven or heavy pot over medium-high heat until melted.
  2. Add beef and cook, turning occasionally, until all sides are seared. Transfer to plate.
  3. Add the remaining bacon fat (or butter) and heat until melted.
  4. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until softened, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add garlic and tomato paste. Cook until fragrant.
  6. Return beef to pot and add barley. Add water, wine, Better Than Bouillon, Worcestershire, thyme and bay leaves.
  7. Bring to boil then reduce to simmer. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cover and simmer with occasional stirring until beef is tender.
  8. Add potatoes and simmer until tender.
  9. Discard thyme and bay leaves. Portion soup into bowls and top with parsley.
  10. Enjoy!
