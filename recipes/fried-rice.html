Fried Rice Recipe

Description

A delicious recipe for fried rice. Perfect to eat as a side or even by itself!

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp full fat, unsalted butter
  • 4 cups cooked plain white rice (chilled)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 shallots, diced
  • 1/2 cup peas, fresh or frozen
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 3-4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Heat 1/2 tbsp of butter in large pan over medium heat until melted.
  2. Add eggs and cook until scrambled. Remove egg, and transfer to bowl.
  3. Add 1 tbsp of butter to pan, heat until melted. Add all vegetables except green onions. Saute until veggies soften.
  4. Increase heat to high and add remaining butter. Stir until melted.
  5. Immediately add rice, sauces and green onions.
  6. Continue sauteing for 3 minutes. Allow rice to rest in bottom of pan between stirs.
  7. Stir in eggs. Remove from heat and stir in sesame oil.
  8. Enjoy!
